## The Secret Skin Care Professionals Don't Always Talk About

Here's something the beauty industry rarely admits: a single facial, no matter how luxurious, can only do so much. Your skin is living tissue — it renews itself on a roughly 28-day cycle, responds to cumulative care, and builds results layer by layer. That's exactly why the Transformation Series at Brasilian Skin Soul was designed around three sessions rather than one.

When Claudia Pieri created this program, she drew from over a decade of advanced esthetic training and a deeply personal philosophy: real skin transformation happens over time, with intention.

## What Is a Transformation Series?

A Transformation Series is a structured, three-session facial program targeting a specific skin concern. Each session builds on the last, working with your skin's natural renewal cycle to produce visible, lasting change — not just a temporary glow that fades by the weekend.

Brasilian Skin Soul currently offers four signature series:

### 1. The Glow Series
Designed for dullness, uneven tone, and lackluster texture. This series combines exfoliation, brightening actives, and hydration layering to reveal your skin's natural radiance. Ideal for anyone whose skin has lost its luminosity due to stress, sun exposure, or aging.

### 2. The Lift & Sculpt Series
Focused on firmness, contouring, and the visible signs of aging. Using advanced techniques that stimulate collagen production and work the facial muscles, this series visibly lifts and tones over the course of three sessions. Popular with clients in the Woodland Hills and greater Los Angeles area who want results without needles or downtime.

### 3. The Clear Skin Series
A targeted approach for congested, acneic, or breakout-prone skin. Rather than simply extracting and moving on, this series works to rebalance oil production, reduce inflammation, and heal active breakouts while preventing future ones.

### 4. The Illuminate Series
The ultimate anti-aging program. This series addresses fine lines, hyperpigmentation, loss of elasticity, and overall skin vitality. Using professional-grade ingredients and layered techniques, the Illuminate Series delivers results that rival more invasive treatments — without recovery time.

## Why Three Sessions?

Think of it this way: one gym session won't transform your body, and one facial won't transform your skin. But three sessions, spaced 2–4 weeks apart to align with your skin's renewal cycle? That's where the magic happens.

**Session one** is about assessment and reset — cleansing, exfoliating, and beginning to address the root issue.

**Session two** goes deeper. Your skin is now primed to receive active ingredients more effectively, and results begin to compound.

**Session three** is where transformation becomes visible. Clients consistently report that this is the session where they look in the mirror and genuinely notice a difference.

## What to Expect

Each session runs approximately 60–75 minutes. You'll receive a personalized skin consultation before your first session, a curated home-care recommendation, and Claudia's ongoing attention throughout your program.

There's no harsh downtime with any of the series — you can return to daily life immediately. Some clients experience light flaking or temporary redness after deeper treatments, but this is normal and brief.
