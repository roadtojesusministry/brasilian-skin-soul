## Not All Facials Are Created Equal

If you've ever walked out of a facial feeling like you just paid for a very expensive nap, you know the disappointment. A great facial isn't just relaxing — it should visibly improve your skin. Finding that in Woodland Hills or anywhere in the San Fernando Valley takes a little know-how.

Here's what to look for, and what to avoid.

## What Separates a Great Facial from a Mediocre One

### 1. A Real Skin Assessment
Before anything touches your face, an expert esthetician should ask about your skin history, current concerns, lifestyle, and products. Your skin is unique — your treatment should be too. A one-size-fits-all approach is the first red flag.

### 2. Professional-Grade Ingredients
The products used during a professional facial should be clinical-strength — not the same formulas you'd find at the drugstore. Look for treatments that include professional exfoliants, active serums (think vitamin C, retinol, hyaluronic acid, AHAs/BHAs), and targeted masks.

### 3. Advanced Techniques, Not Just Relaxation
A truly skilled esthetician applies products strategically, uses professional massage techniques that stimulate circulation and lymphatic drainage, and performs extractions safely and precisely. This is a medical-adjacent skill — not just pampering.

### 4. Results You Can See and Feel
After a great facial in Woodland Hills or Los Angeles, you should notice immediate improvements: brightness, smoothness, reduced puffiness. And with a structured program, those results compound over time.

### 5. Honest Home-Care Guidance
The best estheticians don't just treat you in the chair — they empower you with a realistic home-care plan. They recommend what your skin actually needs, not the most expensive product on the shelf.
